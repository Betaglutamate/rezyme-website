Our Enzyme Microchip

We build a new type of microchip to test millions of enzyme variants enabling AI based enzyme engineering

Advanced Microchip Screening

Our microchip platform is designed for ultra-high-throughput screening. Each chip can analyze millions of individual DNA variants in parallel, using labelled and label-free chemical detection we create the data needed to power AI algorithms.

AI-Powered Enzyme Intelligence

The vast datasets generated by our microchips are fed into Rezyme's sophisticated AI engine. Our AI models identify complex patterns, predict enzyme performance, and guide the design of new enzyme sequences with desired properties like increased activity and stability.

Data & AI Synergy

The combination of our microchip screening and AI-driven design creates a powerful feedback loop. High-quality data from the chip refines our AI models, and smarter AI directs more effective screening and design strategies. This iterative process will unlock the next generation of enzymes and power green chemistry.
